Training Data: ChatGPT has been trained on a massive amount of text data from the internet, which includes books, articles, websites, and more. Multilingual Capabilities: ChatGPT is proficient in multiple languages, making it a versatile tool for communication and assistance in different parts of the world. It can understand and generate text in numerous languages. Contextual Understanding: One of ChatGPT's strengths is its ability to maintain context in a conversation. It can engage in more extended and coherent discussions, unlike earlier chatbots, which often struggled with context and coherence. Diverse Applications: ChatGPT can be used for a wide range of applications, from answering questions and providing explanations to generating creative content, such as stories and poems.
